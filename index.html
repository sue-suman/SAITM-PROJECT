  • * How to Apply

    • Admission Process
    The admission process at SAITM is as follows:
    1. Students must fill an application form by paying an application fee of INR 500/- and submitting the required documents.
    2. Students are required to bring the following documents for verification:
    Graduation Marksheet
    12th Class or Diploma Mark Sheet
    10th Standard Mark Sheet
    Character Certificate
    Migration Certificate
    4 Passport size photographs
    Residence proof for Aadhaar Card
    NEET Score (Latest) for admission
    3. Students are required to register and confirm their seat by depositing a Demand Draft of INR 10,000/- or paying the same amount online
    4.After the institute receives the registration amount, an admission confirmation mail is sent to the candidates as a confirmation of their admission.



  • * Eligibility

    • Admission Eligibility
    B.Sc.(Hons) - A minimum of 50% in Class 12 with Science stream and Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
    B.Sc.(Post Basic) - A certificate in General Nursing and Midwifery and registered as R.N.R.M (Registered Nurse Registered Midwife) with the State Nurses Registration Council.
    M.Sc. - Direct Admissions i.e., admissions are offered based on the merit obtained by students in Bsc Nursing or its equivalent from a recognized board.
